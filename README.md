## 📖 About Me / درباره من

I'm **Mahdi Khoojani**, a 19-year-old software developer from the historic city of **Neyshabur, Iran**. 

My programming journey began in my early teens, fueled by a passion for **Minecraft** and guidance from my uncle. I self-taught **Java** to write custom server plugins and mods, which ultimately sparked my interest in full-stack web development. I focus on writing clean, efficient, and modern code. Outside of tech, I am a high school graduate in experimental sciences and an advocate for open internet access.
